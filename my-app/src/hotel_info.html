About

Location

Watergate Hotel, Located at the heart of Butuan, Watergate Hotel puts you in the threshold of both business and leisure at the same time. This affordably priced modern hotel is conveniently located at the foot of the Butuan City Hall. For the business-minded, government offices, malls and business centers are within 200 meters reach. The airport is only a few minutes’ drive, where we provide complimentary pick-up when arranged in advance.

Watergate Hotel is also the leisure and lifestyle destination of choice within the city. Hosting a commercial centre within the property, you’re never too far from where the action is. The Pick-Me Mini-Mart, its own Lime & Zest Kitchen Restaurant, Cake Miss Cake Shop, AUM meditational Spa Service and many more future offerings will surely keep you coming back for more. Where else in the city can you Share Your Moments and also Share Life? Only at The Watergate Hotel, The Place You’d rather be!

What We Do

We look at the world half full, not half empty. Where some see obstacles, we see opportunities. Beyond filling rooms, we create experience.

Our Capabilities

At Watergate Hotel, we are working with an experienced and innovative management team possessing diverse skills and abilities to understand the unique challenges and complexities involved in creating and preserving values

Dining and Entertainment

There are two on-site restaurants available within the property. Indulge in a symphony of delectable treats from the Lime & Zest Kitchen restaurant. It is a melting pot of different international cuisines from the four corners of the world that will surely wanting you to come back for more. If its authentic dim sum that you are looking for. Look no further because Mandarin Tea Garden will surely satisfy your oriental cravings.

Other Personalized Services

At Watergate, we do our best in providing your wants from a good hotel and make it available at your disposal. Complimentary parking space for all hotel guests, CCTV, and personal safes are provided in each room. You can avail our in-lobby business centre, airport shuttle service, and free WIFI within hotel premises, among other amenities
