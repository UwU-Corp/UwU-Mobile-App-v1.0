An account is a personal or organizational record that allows you to access and manage various services, often requiring authentication through a email and password.

To create an account, click the account button in the right corner and look for a "Login" or "Signup" button. Follow the instructions to enter your information and create a email and password.

If you forget your password, most websites and services offer a "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password" option. Follow the steps to reset your password, often involving an email or SMS verification.

For activities with instant confirmation: You will receive your booking confirmation email within 5 minutes of making your booking.

You can cancel the booking yourself within the cancellation period stated on your booking by following these steps:

  • Click on your profile picture in the top right hand corner of the screen.
  • Go to the Bookings
  • Find the booking you want to cancel and select "Apply for refund"
  • Select refund reason, quantity and then select "Next"

You will then receive an email confirming that your order has been cancelled.
